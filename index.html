web developer pre-boot camp

Hackingtons beta is a HTML/CSS school for beginners, ages 15-99.
We are located in Pleasant Hill, CA.

Online learning sucks, sometimes.

Hackingtons beta is a supplemental program to online tutorials including Code Academy and Code School. We allow you to quickly overcome the initial challenges in learning web development.


We believe coding is a team sport.

We are a real school, with chairs and students. You can give your classmate a high five when you feel like it. Or you can participate in our online community and give virtual high fives outside of class. But thats just silly.


Real tools of the trade, made easy.

There are certain programs that must be mastered from an early stage in your learning, such as Github. These programs aren't taught in most online tutorials. But never fear, we will show you the way.



    The challenging hurdles we focus on:

  • Text-editor and Emmet (brackets.io)
  • GitHub + gh-pages
  • Display:inline-block, floats (and what is a clear-fix)
  • Chrome Devtools
  • Bootstrap
